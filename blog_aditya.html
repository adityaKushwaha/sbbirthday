I have not just known Shreyas for 13 years but been best friends with him all this while. Things have changed around us over the years but I know exactly what he thinks and how he functions. The same set of things that got him excited in 8th standard hold the same value now. For all his extravagant hobbies and ways, I find Bhatia grounded for the same reason.

It's been a while since I have rhymed but here is something that I think is fine;

Sing and laugh about the glorious past,
Here in the moment you give your all but not yet your last.

Resistance will be aplenty and situations will have a catch,
Earn your stars, move fast with a speed in the rough patch,
Yamahas, KTMs and Hondas couldnt match.

Along the way your game gets better and you find a touch like Mesut Ozil,
Surely then you'll find us saying Fo' Shizzle My Nizzle.

Now that you've read it, checkout the first letter of each line, and out them together. Lelz.

Next few years of your life need some serious hustle, you have to rise above a lot of things to put dreams into action. So get to it.
