Find out I did.

I love the sportsperson in him and this isn't limited just to football.

Super passionate: he’ll make time for what he loves no matter what.

Composer: Trusheil, Bobby, Onisho were created in his head.

McDonalds consumer hall of famer.

I love the way he responds to all the names I call him including Benazir.

Animal lover: so much so that he turned non vegetarian to love them in every way.
