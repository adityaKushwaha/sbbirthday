Our Blog.

Smriti Bahl | Sumi

  • Dec 26, 2016

Like many groups in India, we also have a ‘Bhatia’ in our group. However, this man is not the typical ‘Bhatia ji’ that you would imagine. He is a different kind of Bhatia. Our Bhatia.

He oozes out passion for things he loves and displays hunger to explore. He has varied interests and those interests are his religion. He is shy at first but is super comfortable when he gets to know you.

After knowing him for over 10 good years I can confidently say he is one of the best people I have met. We have had our highs, ‘very highs’, lows and ‘very lows’ but I know that if I reach out to him for help, he will be there.

Hope you have a wonderful life ahead and you get what you wish for!

Shreyas 101 for all the ladies out there! I am sure my other friends will have a lot more to add:

1) Let him fly on his bikes, need for speed is in his blood

2) All work and no play, makes shreyas a dull boy

3) He loves his fries, while u are finishing the 1 st chip, the rest will be over. It’s always safe to order your own plate

4) Don’t challenge him when it’s about geography. He will most likely know all the capitals of all the countries.

5) If you borrow a pen from him, make sure you return it otherwise you will hear about it for the rest of your life. No, seriously!

6) Handle with care, he’s a sweet and sensitive kid

7) He will correct your grammar at every chance he gets. Don’t worry! You will get one or two chances here and there too.

8) He takes his games very seriously, even taboo! Take note of that
